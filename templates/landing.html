Preparation

Essential Documents Checklist

Prepare your documents early to ensure smooth and timely applications.

Academic Documents

  • Transcripts / mark sheets
  • Degree / provisional certificates
  • Standardized test scores (SAT, GRE, GMAT)
  • English proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, PTE)

Application Documents

  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Letters of Recommendation (LORs)
  • Updated CV / Resume
  • Portfolio (for design / arts programs)

Financial & Visa

  • Bank statements & financial affidavits
  • Scholarship documents (if any)
  • Passport & ID proofs
  • Visa application forms & photos
